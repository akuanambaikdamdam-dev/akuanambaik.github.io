Synopsis

Tailored specifically for visually driven and creatively inclined people, this guide outlines how artists, writers, and designers can transition their creative passions into sustainable, thriving businesses using visual journaling techniques and creative strategic planning.

Author Background

Lisa Sonora Beam is an American author, artist, business consultant, and registered expressive arts therapist. Holding an MBA alongside extensive art training, she has dedicated her career to coaching creative professionals, visual artists, and corporate teams. She uses art-making as a primary tool for business strategy and personal problem-solving.

Why Read This Book

It bridges the gap between the right-brain creative process and left-brain business operations. It is perfect for individuals who want to monetize their art or freelance careers but feel intimidated by traditional corporate business manuals.
